3UResNet: Explainable AI for Segmentation and Classification of Liver Cirrhosis on T2-Weighted MRI
An explainable deep learning approach for segmentation and classification of liver cirrhosis using T2-weighted MRI.
Samrat Kumar Dey1, Chi-Ren Shyu2, Jonathan T. Barron2, Sofien Bouaziz2, Dan B Goldman2, Steven M. Seitz1,2, Ricardo Martin-Brualla2
1University of Missouri, 2Google Research
Paper arXiv Video Code Data

Abstract

We present 3UResNet, an explainable deep learning framework for liver cirrhosis segmentation and classification using T2-weighted MRI. Our approach leverages 3-level feature extraction and explainability modules to deliver robust and interpretable predictions, achieving state-of-the-art performance on benchmark datasets.
